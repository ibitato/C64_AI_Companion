# Manual Scope Assessment

## Purpose

Define which project areas require dedicated operational manuals and why.

## Scoring Method

Each area is scored from 1 (low) to 5 (high) on:

- Operational risk
- Execution frequency
- Onboarding friction
- Failure impact

Total score range: 4 to 20.

## Assessment Results

| Area | Risk | Frequency | Onboarding | Failure Impact | Total | Decision |
| --- | ---: | ---: | ---: | ---: | ---: | --- |
| Training operations | 5 | 5 | 4 | 5 | 19 | Create dedicated manual |
| Inference packaging | 4 | 4 | 4 | 4 | 16 | Create dedicated manual |
| Release and publishing | 4 | 3 | 3 | 5 | 15 | Create dedicated manual |
| Troubleshooting | 5 | 4 | 5 | 5 | 19 | Create dedicated manual |

## Outcome

Dedicated manuals were created for all areas scoring 15 or above.
